Onmyoji Arena Review – A Combination of Mobile Legend, Arena of Valor, and Vain Glory’s

Update: 10/03/2019, 22:25 PM

Author: Mushroom Tora

Onmyoji Arena is a 5v5 mobile MOBA spinoff of the Japanese-themed turn-based fantasy strategy game Onmyoji. Choose your Shikigami and work together with your team to destroy their towers and secure victory. Publisher: NetEase Games
Playerbase: High
Type: Mobile MOBA
Release Date: July 12, 2018
Pros:
+Beautiful Japanese-themed art and characters.
+Simple controls and mechanics.
+Fast matches.
+Good hero balance.
Cons:
-Limited maps.
-Occasional lag spikes.

Onmyoji Arena Overview


Your favorite Onmyoji characters are now in a MOBA! Engage in classic 5v5 MOBA fights in Onmyoji Arena. Feast your senses on amazing Japanese-themed art, beautifully-modeled 3D characters, and original voice acting. Choose from an ever-growing roster of well-balanced Shikigami inspired by Japanese folklore and the original Onmyoji game. Prepare for battle by equipping your Shikigami with item sets, spells, and Onmyodo that fit your playstyle and, more importantly, your role in the battle. Join the Talent Quest and test your skills against other players of your rank. Win matches to advance in rank and match up with more experienced players. Tired of the 5v5 game mode? Try out the 3v3v3 Royal Battle and face up with two other teams in a race to 30 kills. Earn coins just by joining matches and use them to unlock a wide variety of Shikigami, each with their own unique abilities, playstyles, and backstories. Jump into Onmyoji Arena now and see if you have what it takes to be on top!



There have been a rise of MOBA games since the development of Mobile Legends and Arena of Valor. Developers has been competing with one another to make the best MOBA games. This type of game is played by so many people in the worlds nowadays.
A good graphic, a nice game play, plus a balance heroes are some of the reason why people play the game. NetEase, the developer of RoS (Rules of Survival) and Onmyoji, has made a new MOBA game called Onmyoji Arena.

Description

Onmyoji Arena is part the hit Onmyoji series from NetEase. It builds on the rich character base from the original work to create a new MOBA experience. The game retains the elegant Japanese aesthetics and original voice talents to faithfully recreate Kyoto in the Heian era.
With innovative map designs, a Fog of War mode, and an even larger set of Shikigami skills, players are in for more fun and greater challenges as they battle to reign supreme. The paid amulet system is now replaced by a free Onmyodo system. This levels the playing field. Players from all over the world are connected, and the all-new 3v3v3 Battle Royale will provide players with a fun and novel MOBA experience.
Like any other MOBA game, you will compete in an arena of 5 vs 5. Your goal is to destroy and take controls of the enemies base. The maps is practically the same of any MOBA games, but there is a slight change to the jungle monster.
Like Mobile Legends and AoV, there are 2 jungle boss that will gives you a huge buffs. One is Orochi (which is an 8 headed monster) if you kill it you will get a ghost parade, which will buffs your creeps making them insanely powerful. Kraken, if you kill it, the enemies turret will rendered unusable to easily destroying it.
Onmyoji differs from any others MOBA game:
• First from the graphics, it wins than the other.
• The graphic is so cool and smooth, with a Japan setting making it so cute and anime like.
• The other is the gameplay, although the game is the same as any other MOBA game, it differs in its jungle system. In this game, Jungle monster EXP can’t be shared with one another. Well, another is probably the imbalance heroes.
If you like playing MOBA game, i suggest you to try and play the game. Thank you for reading have a nice day!
